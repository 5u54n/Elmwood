Engaging Education Worth Experiencing

Elmwood Preschool provides children with a safe and secure environment that is filled with love, kindness, and encouragement.

We are located in a quiet residential neighborhood. Our facilities include three cheerfully bright and well-equipped classrooms and a large playground with state-of-the-art equipment.

Committed to Active Learning Fostering Curiousity

Elmwood Christian Preschool has taught and nourished some of the smallest members in the Wellesly community for nearly 40 years. Our integrated Christian curriculum uses a variety of hands-on learning activities as well as themed experiences and units. We incorporate daily opportunities for all types of play, whether it be group, individual, quiet, or active!

Strong Branches, Strong Roots About Our School

Elmwood Christian Preschool is a non-profit organization, and shares a community partnership with Milestone Church.

As a school, we recognize the importance of each child being nurtured, understood, and encouraged. We provide an environment conducive to the optimal spiritual, social, physical and intellectual development of each child.

Fully Licensed For Your Benefit

We are licensed through the Department of Early Education and Care, and through the Town of Wellesley Fire and Building Inspectors. We have three bright, cheerful, well-equipped classrooms and a large playground with state of the art equipment, all located on the church grounds.
